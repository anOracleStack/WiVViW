02\. PLATFORM TOPOLOGY: CORE ENGINES (LOCKED ORDER)  
1\. dRANb (Naming Engine)  
• Workflow: Intake → Generate → Evaluate → Compare → Shortlist → Decide → Export.  
• Intake Model: business\_description, industry\_category, target\_audience, value\_proposition, future\_expansion, competitor\_set, constraints (forbidden\_terms\[\], syllable\_range, style\_notes), risk\_tolerance.  
• 7 Personality Sliders (0-100): Classic/Modern, Minimal/Expressive, Serious/Playful, Technical/Human, Luxury/Accessible, Bold/Calm, Abstract/Literal.  
• Generation Modes (NameSet.type): semantic, phonetic, abstract\_coined, modified\_real, symbolic, ownable\_nearmiss.  
• Anti-Generic Filters: Reject "two keyword SEO" patterns (BestX, MyX, XHub); penalize suffixes (app/pro/hub/ai/ly); penalize category stuffing.  
• Scoring (7 dimensions, 0-100): memorability, pronunciation\_clarity, spelling\_ease (inverse of friction), distinctiveness, brand\_alignment, scalability, collision\_risk\_proxy (inverse; non-legal).  
• Penalty Adjustments (score\_adjustments\[\]):  
    ◦ ambiguous spelling: \-10 to \-35  
    ◦ competitor resemblance: \-15 to \-50  
    ◦ generic keywords: \-10 to \-40  
    ◦ awkward sub-brand expansion: \-10 to \-25  
• Validation Labs: Pronounce Lab (TTS \+ votes), Spell Lab (dictation mismatch), Recall Lab (timed exposure).  
• Next-Galaxy Expansions: Predictive Modeling (Real-Time Trend Analysis, Trend Fit, Future Relevance), Voice Search Optimization (Siri/Alexa legibility).  
2\. brandL (Identity Consolidation Engine)  
• Gate: Post-dRANb; only run when shortlist size is 3–7.  
• Function: Assesses domain/handle availability, scores identity consistency, generates fallback options, Reclaimability & Outreach, Compromise-cost modeling.  
• Next-Galaxy Expansions:  
    ◦ The Hook Engine: Slogan & Tagline Generator (evaluates Memorability and Benefit-Centricity).  
    ◦ Impact Telemetry: Post-launch analytics (Share of Search, Brand Sentiment, Pricing Power, CLV Lift).  
3\. 4IELD (Field Intelligence)  
• Function: Landscape and saturation intelligence. Ingests competitor list (CSV) \+ category tags.  
• Outputs: Clustering \+ saturation \+ conceptual 2D field map \+ risk zones \+ whitespace. Drift index delta on new snapshots.  
• Next-Galaxy Expansions:  
    ◦ Interactive Namescape: 3D Field Visualization (Galaxies of similar names).  
    ◦ Microtrend Detector: Watches emerging hashtags/metaphors.  
    ◦ Sentiment Engine & Morphological Detection.  
4\. 4TRESS (Protection)  
• Function: Non-legal readiness pack. Best-effort class signals, platform-tool viability score.  
• Guardrails: Explicitly "Not legal advice." No cease-and-desist letters; no threats; no guarantees.
