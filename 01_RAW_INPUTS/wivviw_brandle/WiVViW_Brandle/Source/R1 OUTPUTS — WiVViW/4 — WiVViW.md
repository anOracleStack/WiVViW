PART II: THE GENESIS (EXECUTIVE SUMMARY)  
1\. The Vision We are not building "SaaS tools." We are building the Business Metaverse. Current tools (LegalZoom, Squarespace) are fragmented and boring. Brandle OS captures the founder's biometric identity (Voice/Face) and turns it into their primary operating system.  
2\. The "Superman" UX The user is the Director; the AI is the Actor. When you file an LLC, you don't see a form; you see your Digital Twin walking into a government office and stamping the paper. We gamify the boring parts of business into a cinematic experience.  
3\. The 2026 Standard (Google Validated) We align with the industry vision of Agent-to-Agent (A2A) commerce. 6ROXY doesn't just talk to you; it talks to other agents (e.g., AWS, Delta, Stripe) to negotiate deals, refunds, and contracts at light speed using structured data, not human conversation.  
4\. The Governance Layer (Enterprise Trust) We incorporate a "Human-in-the-Loop" Control Plane. No agent takes legal or financial action without a "Permission Envelope." This de-risks the autonomy for investors and regulators.  
5\. The Partnership Economy If a user develops an idea or product while using our platform, we treat it as a Partnership (Revenue Share/Co-Development), not a seizure. We facilitate internal business (e.g., connecting a Roofer to a Real Estate Agent) and take a commission on the transaction.

Position: Brand protection assistant (non-legal) Boundaries: Informational only; no legal conclusions; no enforcement language.  
CORE FLOW

2\. Readiness Assessment: Distinctiveness signals, Confusion risk (user-provided), Use evidence check.  
3\. Class Suggestion Intelligence: Best-effort mapping of plain language to class clusters.

5\. Form Generation: Structured text for ownership, goods/services, and evidence logs.  
6\. Post-Filing Awareness: Monitoring reminders and renewal checkpoints.  
7\. Enforcement Viability Scoring: Scores usability of platform tools (0-100).  
SCORING MODEL (MATHEMATICAL LOGIC)  
From 4TRESS\_design.txt  
• Distinctiveness Score (0–1): Fanciful (1.0) \> Arbitrary (0.9) \> Suggestive (0.7) \> Descriptive (0.4) \> Generic (0.0).  
• Saturation Risk (0–1): Relative density of similar marks. High density \= High risk.  
• Class Density Score (0–1): Measure of registrations in chosen class. Open \= High score.  
• Evidence Quality Score (0–1): Completeness of specimens/docs.  
• Filing Basis Weight: Use-in-commerce (+0.1), Intent-to-use (+0.05).  
• FORMULA: Enforcement Viability \= 40% Distinctiveness \+ 30% (1 – Saturation) \+ 20% Class Density \+ 10% Evidence Quality \+ Filing Basis Weight  
    ◦ Bands: High (\>0.75), Moderate (0.5–0.75), Low (\<0.5).
